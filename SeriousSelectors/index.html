Thank you for visiting my page! Hope you liked my game :)

The rules of the game are simple! Take turns moving your pieces diagonally forward. Capture a piece from your opponent by leaping over their piece diagonally and landing on the other side. You can chain multiple jumps together if the pieces line up. If one of your pieces reaches the opponent side's end, it can start to go backwards. The first person to lose their pieces loses.
